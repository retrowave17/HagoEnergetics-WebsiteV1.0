High Capacity Scaling 02

Continuous Slow Pyrolysis

Utilizes robust, continuous rotary drum reactors operating strictly between 400°C and 600°C with highly controlled residence times (15–45 minutes). This process balances carbon retention parameters to deliver an optimal, massive 30% to 40% solid material carbon yield.

Strategic Goal: Delivers the extreme bulk volume required to supply price-sensitive concrete and infrastructure aggregate markets.
Advanced Material Refinery 03

Advanced Flash Pyrolysis

Suspends highly uniform, pulverized biomass inside vertical, fluid beds of silica sand operating at rapid high-temperature points (600°C to 800°C). Near-instantaneous thermal transfer yields unmatched physical properties with zero residual volatile organic compounds (VOCs).

Strategic Goal: Engineers premium sorbent media with 130+ m²/g surface areas while co-generating clean energy for the grid.
